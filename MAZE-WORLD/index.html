• Allows to click to add block and to walk through any block (water, wall, other entities, etc...)

  • Controls

  • WASD - Change direction

  • Arrows - Walk

  • WASD/Arrows +Alt - Use Down Left/Right on Hexagon

  • 1,2,3,4 - Dig, Place, Boat, Map

  • Q/E - Rotate Left/Right

  • R/R+Alt - Rotate Picked up Blocks Clock/Counte-Clock wise

  • F - Use Item

  • Shift - Change Polygon

  • Space - Pass Time
